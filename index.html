What's New

  • Building Character & Competence

    We at Glendale have a twin focus on building Character and Competence of our Glendalites. We are a Multiple Intelligence school, and we believe that “the more ways we teach, the more children we reach.” We aim at
    1. Developing desirable traits such as responsibility, generosity and care
    2. Innovative teaching methods that help them reach their highest potential.
    Our campus culture of RRC-Respect, Responsibility and Cooperation along with the integration of United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and Leader in Me in our curriculum, help to sow the seeds of Character in young minds.

    We at Glendale love to challenge the odds by stimulating our students' minds by improving
    1. Scientifically designed curriculum

    4. Multiple Intelligence is integrated into our curriculum to develop the innate potential of each child. We believe “the more ways we teach the more children we reach.
